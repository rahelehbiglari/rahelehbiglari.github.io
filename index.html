About Me

Since 2021, I have been a Ph.D. researcher in the Cosys-Lab research group at the University of Antwerp under the supervision of Prof.dr. Joachim Denil.

My PhD research topic is:

Foundations for Self-Adaptive Abstraction and Approximation in Digital Twins with Real-time Requirements

My research interests are:

Modeling and Simulation,
Digital Twins Engineering,
Cyber-physical systems,
Adaptive Systems,
Real-Time Systems,
Algorithms
, Software Engineering
, and I am open to broadening my knowledge in other interesting topics in Computer Engineering.
